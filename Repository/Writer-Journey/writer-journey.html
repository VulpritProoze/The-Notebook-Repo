Did you know that...

...Beethoven passed away on March 26, 1827 after a long illness? His friend compared the moment to the composer’s symphonies with “crashes that sound like hammering on the portals of Fate.” Nobody knows for certain about what Beethoven’s final words are, although his friends and biographers have recorded many different versions including “Pity, pity — too late,” or “Applaud, my friends, the comedy is over” and “I shall hear in heaven,” a reference to his deafness.

Source: Medium, "Five Facts You Probably Didn’t Know About Beethoven”

What is the Writer's Journey?

Perhaps one of my greatest passion lies in writing. I love to imbue fantasy elements to any object I seem to take interest on, along with giving it an origin story. Though, as with most writers, my procrastination knows no bounds. I just can't seem to wrap my head around trying to at least achieve what I really want to do, which is to finally start damn writing! As such, I have taken the initiative to at least keep track of my progress, and that is the purpose of the Writer's Journey.
