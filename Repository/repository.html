What is The Repository?

The Repository's sole purpose is for me to be able to express the fullest potential of my very being. At times, mediocre content may be submitted here, but I believe that even small things will amount to such that it makes up to be significant to the greater whole. The Repository is also a significant leap for my personal development as a whole because I've never been this outgoing before. To share my creative pursuits to everyone is very thrilling, likewise nerve-wracking. The Repository consists of three sections: The Fact Dump, The Creative Dump, and lastly the Writer's Journey.
